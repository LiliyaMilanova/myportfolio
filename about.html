Why Designer?

Hello, I am Lilia and I got my education at the National Academy of Arts in the subject of Poster and Visual Communication. My interests are art, design and technology.

For several years I combined the positions of a website administrator and designer in an online shop moebebe. For me, the job was a challenge that allowed me to develop experience in web design, photography and video processing. I received a valuable experience in communicating with clients and partners, as well as building the face of the brand on social networks. I realized I was very excited, when I have to write html and css code, so I decided to improve my skills with the courses of Software University. Currently my interests are mainly in the UX and UI projects.

For me, the biggest challenge in design is to create exciting content that can cure someone's pain. My goal is to find a position in an organization that will allow me to continue learning and following my passion for design.
